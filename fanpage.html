The Fantastic Beatles

Bio

The Beatles were a famous rock band from the 60's, formed in Liverpool, England by John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Pete Best.

Even though the band dealt with a lot of controversy, they were around for 10 years. They played their last performance on the rooftop of the building for their multimedia company, Apple Corps, in January 1969.
